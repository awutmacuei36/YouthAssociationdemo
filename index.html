About KYA-Juba

The Kuei Youth Association - Juba Branch was established in 2015 to unite and empower young people from Lakes State living in South Sudan's capital. Our organization serves as a platform for youth development, cultural preservation, and community engagement.

With over 500 active members, we work to address the challenges facing our youth while celebrating our rich cultural heritage and contributing to national development.

Our Mission & Vision

  • To empower youth through education and skills development
  • To preserve and promote our cultural heritage
  • To foster peace, unity, and leadership among youth
  • To contribute to community development initiatives
  • To advocate for youth rights and opportunities
